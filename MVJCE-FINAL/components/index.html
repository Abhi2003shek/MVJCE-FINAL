Department News

About MVJCE

    Established in 1982, the MVJ College of Engineering, the flagship of Venkatesha Education Society, imparts education beyond engineering and management. Situated on a 15-acre campus in Whitefield, Bangalore, MVJCE has state-of-the-art infrastructure, creating an environment for progressive learning and development. We are permanently affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). MVJCE has on campus learning centers in collaboration with companies such as National Instruments & Infosys. Most of our programs are accredited by National Board of Accreditation.

About Department of CSE & ISE

     The Department of Computer science and Engineering was started in the year 1985. CSE department is affliated to VTU, Approved by AICTE and Accredited by NBA. Department has full time UG program in Computer Science and Engineering and PG Program in Computer Science and Engineering. Department conducts regular interactive workshops, seminars, conferences,intra collegiate competitions for technical skill enhancements and many other programs. The department has well experienced and highly qualified faculty members, who have specialized in the latest areas of in Computer science and Engineering, which includes, Cloud computing, Big data, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Learning,Under water communication, Semantic learning, Image Processing, Wireless Network Security, Computer Networks, and so on. The Department of Information Science & Engineering was started in the year 1999. The Information Science & Engineering Department at MVJCE endeavours to provide high quality technical education, research and innovation, for students and faculty to meet the ever-growing needs of industry and society. The Department emphasizes on quality and high standards and provides an all-round personality development with responsibility. Through its strong industry links, ties to professional bodies, and state - of the-art laboratory facilities, the Department of Information Science & Engineering truly stands tall. The Department is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, approved by AICTE, New Delhi,recognized by UGC with 2(f) & 12(B) status, and accredited by NBA and NAAC.

About Conference

     The aim of the National Conference on Innovative Research in Computer Science and Information Technology is to bring together the academicians, researchers, scientists, engineers, technocrats, students and industry professionals from various institutions, universities, and industries to share and discuss the ideas, technologies and research findings. The purpose of Conference is to promote research and also to ignite young research oriented and innovative brains in the field of

  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data
  • IOT
  • Cyber security and Block Chaining
  • Networking
  • Networking
  • Cloud Computing

     This Conference expands its coverage in the specified areas where expert talks and paper presentations will be held to inspire the young researchers and keep them motivated. Thus, the main objectives of the Conference are:

  • To endow opportunities for delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences to explore and share innovative work carried out by individuals.
